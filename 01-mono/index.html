01About

I've spent the last eight years working across the stack — building product features, untangling legacy systems, and obsessing over the developer experience of the tools my teammates rely on every day.

My sweet spot is the boundary between frontend and platform: design systems, build tooling, observability, and the API contracts that hold everything together. I care about code that's boring in the best way — predictable, well-tested, and easy to delete.
